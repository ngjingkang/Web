What is Banana Bread?

Banana bread is a very popular batter-based quick bread. As the name implies, quick breads are easy to make. They are leavened with baking powder and steam produced in the oven. Because there is no yeast, no fermentation time is necessary.

  1. Banana bread formulations are not as rich as regular batter cakes.
  2. For this reason, this type of sweet bread is usually consumed during breakfast.

Origin

The origin of the first banana bread recipe is unknown, though some speculate it was originated in the 18th century by housewives experimenting with pearlash. The home baking revival of the 1960s and the simplicity of its recipe led to an explosion in banana bread's popularity.
