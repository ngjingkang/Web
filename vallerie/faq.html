Shome FAQ

Product Payment Delivery Storage

Product

Do the Shome products contain lactose or gluten?

All Shome Bakery products are 100% plant-based and therefore dairy free, naturally lactose-free and free from animal protein. The majority of the products are also gluten free. Always check the ingredient lists on the package for the most recent and detailed information.

Does Shome Bakery use stabilisers and thickeners?

Yes, ice cream needs to be velvety, desserts have to hug the spoon and linger in the mouth. Basically, we want our foods to taste as good as at the moment we made them. That’s where stabilisers and thickeners come in, helping our desserts stay deliciously thick, every time you take them out of the fridge. The stabiliser and thickener use by Shome Bakery are guar gum, pectin, locust bean gum, xanthan gum and much more.

How much sugar is in Shome bakery products?

Shome offers a wide range of food, containing variable quantities of sugar from unsweetened and low sugar products, through to gently sweetened, indulgent products.

Does Shome use colourings?

Yes, the colouring used is entirely of colours from natural plant-based foods. So we add a few drops of (natural) colourings or food extracts to restore any colour lost in cooking or to add a little extra zing to your dessert. For example, anthocyanins, caratenes ,curcumin and etc.

Payment

What form of payment method do Shome Bakery offers?

We accept Credit Card (Visa, Master, American Express), Debit Card, and Online Bank Transfer. All the transaction information will not be kept with Shome.com.my.

How do I know if my order and payment has been successfully went through?

All successful order will receive a confirmation email and message with your order number and details. Do check your junk/spam mail box if it’s not in your inbox.

Delivery

It there any charges for delivery?

Delivery fee is FREE for purchase above RM150. Delivery fee charged by Shome Bakery depends on the location. You can always check the delivery fee while forming your order.

Can I specify the exact delivery time?

You are able to select your most preferred delivery time slot when you checkout . However, we are unable to provide exact time delivery as it’s subjected to traffic condition and weather.

Do you do same day delivery and how many day of advance order required for a delivery?

Yes, same day delivery is available (except customised product) but orders must be placed by 4 p.m. daily.

How do I track my order or check if my order has been delivered?

You will receive an email or SMS notification one day before to your delivery confirming your delivery and a tracking number with which you can check the status of your order.

Can I self-collect instead of deliver to me?

Yes, you may self-collect from our closest collection point to you if you wish to. You may check out our closest branch from our website or through our live chat.

Storage

How should I store Shome Bakery products?

All the cakes must keep refrigerated (2℃ ‑ 6℃) at all time before served. Cookies should keep in dry room temperature, avoid drom sun contact. The other products should be stored in the fridge if left overnight.

Can I freeze Shome products for storage?

Yes, our product can easily be stored in the freezer as long as you stick to the general freezing guidelines, for example, try our ice cream! If our products are not incorporated in a recipe, we do not recommend freezing them. Although freezing doesn’t affect the safety, or nutritional value of the product in any way, we cannot guarantee the same high quality standard in taste and texture after unfreezing the product.
